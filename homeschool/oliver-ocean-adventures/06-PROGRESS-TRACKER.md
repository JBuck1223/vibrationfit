## Math Milestones

**Counting ceiling:** Week 1 ______ → Week 4 ______ (goal: toward 120)

- ☐ Counts on from any given number ("start at 26")
- ☐ Counts backward from 20+; skip counts by 2s, 5s, 10s
- ☐ Compares two-digit numbers (Shark Chomp — chomps the bigger)
- ☐ Addition/subtraction facts within 10 getting automatic
- ☐ Solves story problems within 20 with objects
- ☐ Missing-number problems (8 + ? = 12)
- ☐ Knows fact families (6+3, 3+6, 9−3, 9−6)

- ☐ Builds two-digit numbers as tens and ones (Shell Bank)
- ☐ Adds one-digit to two-digit numbers with objects (26 + 5)
- ☐ Ten more / ten less without recounting
- ☐ Measures with a ruler in inches; orders three lengths
- ☐ Tells time to hour and half-hour (analog + digital)
- ☐ Sorts daily activities into AM / PM
- ☐ Identifies penny, nickel, dime, quarter and values
- ☐ Pays an exact amount two different ways
- ☐ Names pentagon, hexagon, octagon, rhombus, trapezoid; counts sides and vertices
- ☐ Makes and interprets a tally chart
