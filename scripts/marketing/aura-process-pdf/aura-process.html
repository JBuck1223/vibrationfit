1

Awareness — Recognizing the Vibration

Awareness is the first step to shifting your vibration. In this step, we bring conscious attention to your current vibration and the way that it feels.

Questions to ask yourself:
  • What emotions am I currently experiencing? (Refer to the Emotional Guidance Scale)
  • Do I feel above or below the Green Line?
  • Do I have clarity on what situation, thought, or belief is contributing to this feeling?
  • Would I like to continue carrying this vibration forward?
Example

"I feel anxious about money. Every time I look at my credit card balance, my stomach drops. This feels below the Green Line. If I continue holding onto this, I will keep feeling stressed and stuck. I don't want to stay in this vibration."

2

Unplug — Disengaging the Old Vibration

Now that you are aware, it's time to step back from identifying with the emotion. You are not your thoughts. You are not your emotions. You are the observer.

Questions to ask yourself:
  • Can I imagine myself as an observer, simply watching this thought without engaging with it?
  • What would happen if I decided to let go of the story that's perpetuating this vibration?
  • How does it feel to take a step back and recognize that I am not my thoughts?
Example

"I am aware that I feel anxious because of the way I am focused on my debt. The balance is just a number — it is not who I am. I choose to consciously unplug from this energy by shifting my focus elsewhere whenever the money stress comes up."

3

Replace — Choosing a New Direction

Now that you have unplugged from the old vibration, it's time to introduce a new one.

Questions to ask yourself:
  • What is a better-feeling thought I can choose instead?
  • What is the best-case scenario in this situation?
  • What emotion would I like to feel instead?
  • Can I think of a time when I felt this way before? What did that feel like?
  • How can I shift my focus toward a more empowering perspective?
Example

"Instead of focusing on what I owe, I can choose to focus on the money that is already flowing to me. The best-case scenario is that surplus keeps showing up and my Safe Number grows month after month. I want to feel abundant and at ease. I remember a time when money felt easy — and it felt amazing."

4

Activate — Reinforcing the New Vibration

Now that you have chosen a new perspective, you must activate it through repetition and focus.

How to activate:
  • Use activation cycles — holding the new thought for at least 68 seconds at a time — to solidify the new vibrational signature.
  • Intentionally activate the new thought pattern anytime you become aware that the old default pattern is engaged. Repeat this until your chosen thought pattern is your new default.
Example

"I will take three deep breaths and remind myself that I am in control of my energy. I will repeat: 'God's wealth is circulating in my life! His wealth cascades to me in avalanches of abundance.' I will visualize (for at least 68 seconds at a time) my Safe Number sitting in my account and my balances shrinking. I will check in with myself throughout the day — especially when I pay bills — to keep activating my chosen vibration."

You can't U-turn at 70 mph. If you're heading the wrong direction at full speed, you can't safely flip it instantly — and vibration works exactly the same way. A.U.R.A. is how you slow down, turn around, and build speed in the direction you actually want to go.

The Money Framework:
From $100K in the Hole to $100K in the Bank

This is the flow that carried us out of six figures of debt — very simple, but very powerful. The target at every step: vibrational harmony with the infinite abundance already available to you.

1

Determine your Safe Number

This is the number that, if it were sitting in the bank, would make you actually feel safe for a minimum of 90 days — even if no new money came in.

2

Go to minimums on all debt

Instead of trying to allocate more money to debt, begin by making the absolute minimum payment on everything — just enough to keep it from getting larger. This is especially true for high-interest credit cards.

3

Tune to the vibration of more than enough

The universe is infinitely abundant — there is always more available to you than you are currently letting in, because there is just always more available! Get into vibrational harmony with additional abundance — whether it's $1, $100, $1,000, or $10,000 — and look for any way to create a surplus beyond what you normally bring in.

4

Build to your Safe Number

Allocate all additional money — everything beyond your absolute spending and your minimums — to your Safe Number until you reach it.

5

Sweep the high-interest debt — monthly

Once your Safe Number is in place, stay above that line. Then, at the end of each month, once all your payments are satisfied, sweep everything above your Safe Number to debt. Above 5% is high interest; below 5% is low interest (we'll deal with that later). Sweep to the smallest balance first, killing cards and obligations one at a time, until all high-interest debt is gone!

6

Escalate to your Super Safe Number

You'll be tempted to start paying down the low-interest debt for that amazing feeling of being completely debt-free — but not yet. First, escalate your Safe Number to a minimum of 6 months of feeling safe, up to 12 months depending on your family situation. For many people this lands somewhere around $30,000–$50,000. Keep making normal payments on all low-interest debt until you reach it.

7

Sweep again — and go completely debt-free

With your Super Safe Number in place, perform the same sweeping process to eliminate all low-interest debt — student loans, car payments, all of it. When you owe no one in the world anything and you are completely liquid, you hit a magical pivot point.

Our numbers

Our first Safe Number — back when it was just the two of us, no children — was $10,000. We held that number solid and swept everything left at the end of each month exactly as described above. Our Super Safe Number was $50,000; once we reached it, we swept away the remaining student debt, car payments, and every last low-interest balance. Then the pivot: completely liquid and debt-free, cash began popping like crazy — the first $100,000 was quickly in hand, and it grew to $250,000 and beyond. That base cash put 20% down on our dream home, paid cash for a brand-new Honda Odyssey Elite, and set us and our kids up for a life of freedom and fun.

No matter where you are, we truly believe a vibrational shift can be made in your life. Reality is subjective, so your experience will be your own — but the target is always vibrational harmony with infinite abundance. As our incantation says: all of our needs, desires and goals are met instantaneously by infinite intelligence, for we are one with God and God is everything. Love and light to you, from us! We look forward to hearing about your transformation as you step into the life you choose.
