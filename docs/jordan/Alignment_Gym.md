**How to structure the weekly live event**
Name:
“Weekly Vibe Alignment Session”

Promise:
“Bring your wins, wobbles, and questions. Leave with your next most aligned action.”

Format (60 minutes):

10 min – Wins & synchronicities share
15 min – Short teaching or guided activation tied to a core feature (audios, journaling, Vision Board, Vibe Tribe)
30 min – Hot‑seat coaching / Q&A
5 min – Next‑week intention + homework (post format for Vibe Tribe)
This hits:

Community linking (people see each other)
Product usage (you keep pointing back into the app)
Emotional support (they don’t feel alone when constraints hit)
You can even treat attendance as an “engagement metric” the same way I tracked gym visits against churn.
